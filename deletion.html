Account Deletion Request for ServiceReport

In compliance with Google Play's data privacy policies, we provide ServiceReport users with a direct and transparent way to request the deletion of their account and all associated data.

Developer: Elier Viera (ejv96.github.io)

Steps to request account deletion

  1. Send an email directly to the developer at: ejvapps.online@gmail.com.
  2. Use the following subject line: ServiceReport Account Deletion Request.
  3. In the message body, clearly state the email address you used to register and sync your data within the application. You do not need to provide your OTP code or password.

Data types that will be deleted and retention policy

Once your request is processed via email, the following data associated with your account will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our cloud synchronization systems:

  • Your registered email address.
  • All service reports, monthly hours, and study notes that have been synchronized and stored in the cloud.

Once the process is complete, no copies of this data will be retained on our servers. Please note that data stored locally on your device is not affected by this process; to remove it completely from your device, you must uninstall the application or clear the cache/data for ServiceReport through your Android device settings.

Processing Period: Your deletion request will be processed and completed within a maximum of 48 hours upon receipt of the email.
